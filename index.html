Building an Inclusive Senior Wellness Community in Elizabeth

Halal-Friendly Senior Community Center of Elizabeth Inc. is an IRS-recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit currently developing inclusive senior wellness, nutrition, and social engagement programs for older adults in Elizabeth, New Jersey. Our future programming is designed to serve seniors of all backgrounds, with culturally responsive services, including halal meal options, to reduce barriers and promote dignity and wellbeing.

Our Mission

Serving Seniors with Dignity, Inclusion, and Care

We are currently in the planning phase, building a community-centered senior wellness program that promotes social connection, access to nutritious meals, and overall wellbeing. Our goal is to create a welcoming environment where older adults feel respected, supported, and connected.

While our programs are inclusive and open to all seniors, we are intentionally incorporating culturally responsive elements, including halal meal options, to ensure equitable access for underserved communities.

🥘
